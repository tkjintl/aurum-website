I · PATRON ONE-PAGER

One kilogram. Three doors.

Physical gold is the qualifier. Private capital access is the product. What follows is what a Founders patron will receive at launch, by tier.

WHAT YOU HOLD

What you hold.

A Founders patron holds LBMA-grade physical gold in allocated custody at Malca-Amit Singapore Freeport. The bars are in the patron's name, by serial number, insured under a Lloyd's of London syndicate.

The gold also serves as collateral for a credit line at a Singapore institutional bank. Leverage is tiered to commitment — 50% at Founders I, 65% at II, 75% at III. The metal never moves; the credit it unlocks deploys into Aurum Funds.

WHAT AURUM DOES WITH IT

What Aurum does with it.

Aurum Funds deploys the credit into two equal sleeves: pre-IPO growth equity (Series D+ AI, robotics, critical infrastructure) and US mid-market private credit (10–14% gross, with equity participation where structure allows). Reinvestment is the default path — fund-level returns compound across vintages.

WHAT YOU RECEIVE

What you receive.

Direct allocation in Aurum Fund deal flow. Curated pre-IPO equity and US private credit that Korean structures cannot reach directly. A kilogram of physical gold in your name, working as collateral while it appreciates. One Singapore subscription replacing the FSCMA, FETA, KoFIU, and NTS paperwork that compounds across every direct deal. Quarterly reporting and full portal access are standard.

II · MECHANISM EXPLAINER

One kilogram. Two jobs.

A single kilogram, at rest in a Malca-Amit vault, simultaneously does two jobs — it protects the patron, and it funds the institutional deal pipeline through a tiered credit line (up to 50% / 65% / 75% LTV by tier, subject to bank discretion). No bank, no family office, no product in Asia combines the two. Aurum provides that form.

The bar protects.

Protection · unmoved · unseized

Allocated and segregated at Malca-Amit SG Freeport. Serial-identified. Insured under a Lloyd's syndicate. The patron's name on the certificate. LTV monitoring with patron alerts at 70% — 10 percentage points before the bank's margin threshold. The metal is still, and it is safe.

A credit line from a Singapore institutional bank, collateralized by the bar — up to 50% / 65% / 75% LTV by tier. Proceeds deploy into pre-IPO equity and US private credit. ~15% base IRR target, net of fees.

ENGINE I · GOLD FLOOR

Compounds at the rate of money supply.

The kilogram appreciates passively against won, dollar, yen — the asymmetry that makes Korean families hold it in the first place. Aurum doesn't accelerate it. It simply stops the metal from sitting idle.

ENGINE II · THE PIPELINE

Compounds at the rate of the deal book.

The credit drawn against the bar deploys into pre-IPO equity at 18–25% and private credit at 10–14% gross. The bar grows the floor; the deals grow the ceiling.

The metal sits still. The capital works. The NAV is the sum.

VI · A NOTE

A note, at first close.

To the founding group —

You have read the package. If you are still reading, it is because you already know how we move, and you have, at times, felt how far that work sits from you. This is the structure we built to close that distance.

Years of sourcing across the US, Asia, and Europe — direct-LP relationships, crossover rounds, secondaries that clear only through a returned call — taught us where Korean capital actually breaks. Not on opportunity. On the route to it: a regulatory perimeter that compounds with every deal, ninety-day pre-approvals on two-week windows. Patrons missed positions they should have been in, while gold held for a generation sat earning nothing.

That is when the gold became the answer. Not as a thesis on metal — though we hold one — but as the mechanism that solves the route. Allocated in your name in Singapore, insured, untouched. An institutional credit line that puts the bar to work without moving it. A MAS-regulated subscription that routes pro-rata into positions our network has already sourced. The metal you would hold anyway becomes the qualifier that opens rooms Seoul cannot reach directly.

Our ability to source continues to compound — Anthropic and Shield AI are what that pipeline produces today, a snapshot of a flow that does not slow down. The patrons who sit inside the founding group see it first.

We set the floor at a kilogram by design. The room is the asset. The patrons who belong in it tend to know each other already, and the seats — counted, by tier — will not be reopened.

The book opens this quarter. When you are ready, we are ready.
