I · ORIGIN · HOW WE BEGAN
ORIGIN

The deals were there.
We built the mechanism.

The principals have backgrounds in US private market investing and Korean cross-border capital placement. The capital was ready. The deals were available. What was missing was the mechanism to hold them.

FRICTION

Demand outgrew the structures built to serve it.

Cross-border appetite for offshore private positions rose faster than the channels designed to receive it. Each direct deal triggered another regulatory filing, another AML review, another year-end line on the member's overseas account return. The friction compounded with every deal.

ACCESS

Institutional access did not arrive by accident.

The general partners that clear the kind of deal worth doing — the closed primaries, the curated secondaries, the disciplined credit books — work through long relationships, not broker channels. The right counterparties were not advertising.

II · MEMBER ONE-PAGER

One kilogram. The Founding 100.

Physical gold is the qualifier. Private capital access is the product. What follows is what each member will receive at launch.

WHAT YOU HOLD

What you hold.

A member holds LBMA-grade physical gold in allocated custody at Singapore Freeport custodian. The bars are in the member's name, by serial number, insured under the custodian's standard insurance programme.

The gold also serves as collateral for a credit line at a Singapore institutional bank — up to 75% LTV, per LP agreement parameters and sized to commitment level. The metal never moves; the credit it unlocks deploys into Aurum Funds.

WHAT AURUM DOES WITH IT

What Aurum does with it.

Aurum Funds deploys the credit into two sleeves: pre-IPO growth equity (Series D+ AI, robotics, critical infrastructure) and US mid-market mid-market private credit (mid-single to low-double-digit gross target, with equity participation where structure allows). Reinvestment is the default path — fund-level returns compound across vintages.

WHAT YOU RECEIVE

What you receive.

Direct allocation in Aurum Fund deal flow. Curated pre-IPO equity and US private credit not commonly accessible through standard Korean retail channels. A kilogram of physical gold in your name, working as collateral while it appreciates. One Singapore subscription consolidating Singapore-side per-position reporting at the Fund level across every direct deal. Quarterly reporting and full portal access are standard.

III · MECHANISM EXPLAINER

One kilogram. Two jobs.

A single kilogram, at rest in a Singapore Freeport vault, simultaneously does two jobs — it protects the member, and it funds the institutional deal pipeline through a tiered credit line (up to 75% LTV, per LP agreement parameters and sized to commitment level). No bank, no family office, no product in Asia combines the two. Aurum provides that form.

JOB ONE

The bar protects.

Protection · unmoved · unseized

Allocated and segregated at Singapore Freeport. Serial-identified. insured under the custodian's standard insurance programme. The member's name on the certificate. LTV monitoring with member alerts at 70% — 10 percentage points before the bank's margin threshold. The metal is held in allocated, segregated custody — subject to custody agreement terms, insurance policy conditions (including per-event limits and exclusions), and applicable Singapore insolvency law. See PPM §14 for full custody, insurance, and segregation terms.

JOB TWO

The credit earns.

Production · deployed · productive

A credit line from a Singapore institutional bank, collateralized by the bar — up to 75% LTV, per LP agreement parameters and sized to commitment level. Proceeds deploy into pre-IPO equity and US private credit. ~15% base IRR target · new fund vehicle, net of fees · illustrative.

ENGINE I · GOLD FLOOR

Compounds at the rate of money supply.

The kilogram appreciates passively against won, dollar, yen — the asymmetry that makes families across Asia hold it in the first place. Aurum doesn't accelerate it. It simply stops the metal from sitting idle.
